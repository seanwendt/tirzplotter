Plasma Concentration Estimator

One-compartment PK model with first-order absorption & elimination. Parameters from FDA NDA 215866 Clinical Pharmacology Review (Eli Lilly, 2022). Body weight is recorded per injection for accurate longitudinal PK scaling.

⚠ For educational/informational use only. This tool does not constitute medical advice. Individual PK varies substantially (FDA-reported CV ~22% for Cmax/AUC). Never adjust your dosing based on this model without consulting your prescribing physician.

Current Est. i
Current Estimated Concentration The model's estimate of tirzepatide plasma concentration at this moment, computed by summing contributions from all logged injections at today's date and time. Each dose uses its own recorded body weight for scaling.
—
Peak (Cmax) i
Peak Concentration (Cmax) The highest plasma concentration in the displayed window. Tirzepatide typically peaks 24–72 hours post-injection (tmax). With repeated weekly dosing, peaks increase due to drug accumulation — reaching ~1.7× the first-dose peak at steady state, per FDA accumulation ratio data.
—
Trough (Cmin) i
Trough Concentration (Cmin) The lowest concentration between doses, measured after the first full dose cycle (post-tmax of dose 1). A non-zero trough reflects tirzepatide's long 5-day half-life — drug from prior injections is still present before the next dose is given.
—
SS Reached i
Steady State (SS) Steady state is when drug absorbed per dose equals drug eliminated per dosing interval — so concentrations stabilize into a consistent repeating pattern. Detected here when consecutive pre-dose trough values change by less than 5% between cycles. Per FDA NDA 215866, tirzepatide reaches SS after approximately 4 weeks of once-weekly dosing.
